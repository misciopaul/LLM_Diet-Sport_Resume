# Meditation Roadmap: From 0 to Hero 🧘‍♂️

This roadmap is designed to gradually guide you from a "zero" level to integrating meditation as a pillar of your day, reaching 4 daily sessions.

## 🏁 Phase 1: The Basics (Week 1)
**Goal:** Create a daily anchor.
- **Frequency:** Once a day (preferably in the morning or before bed).
- **Duration:** 5 minutes.
- **Focus:** Simple observation of the breath.
- **Hero Check:** Did you complete at least 5 out of 7 days? Move to Phase 2.

## 📈 Phase 2: Building the Habit (Week 2-3)
**Goal:** Solidify the practice and add a second session.
- **Frequency:** Twice a day (Morning + Evening).
- **Duration:** 5-10 minutes per session.
- **Focus:** Body Scan in the evening, focused breathing in the morning.
- **Hero Check:** Do you feel comfortable staying still for 10 minutes? Move to Phase 3.

## 🔄 Phase 3: Integration (Week 4-5)
**Goal:** Use meditation to reset your day.
- **Frequency:** 3 times a day (Morning + Post-Lunch + Evening).
- **Duration:** 10-15 minutes in the morning/evening, 5 minutes after lunch.
- **Focus:** Awareness of thoughts (without judgment).
- **Hero Check:** Does the post-lunch session help you manage the afternoon energy slump? Move to Phase 4.

## 🏆 Phase 4: Hero (Week 6+)
**Goal:** Constant mindful presence.
- **Frequency:** 4 times a day (Morning, Post-Lunch, Evening, Pre-Sleep).
- **Duration:**
  - **Morning:** 15 min (Intention and Focus)
  - **Post-Lunch:** 5-10 min (Reset and Presence)
  - **Evening:** 10-15 min (Decompression)
  - **Pre-Sleep:** 5-10 min (Deep Relaxation/Gratitude)

---
> [!TIP]
> Do not rush. If a phase feels difficult, stay there for another week. Consistency is more important than duration.
